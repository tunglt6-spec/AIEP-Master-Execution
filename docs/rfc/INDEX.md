# AIEP — Requests for Comments (RFC)

RFCs record significant design decisions for the AI Engineering Platform, with their
motivation, detailed design, drawbacks, alternatives and open questions.

## Index

| RFC | Title | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [0001](./RFC-0001-review-level-policy.md) | Review Level Policy (L1–L4) and the Codex-L4-only Token-Preservation Guard | Accepted |

## Conventions

- Filename: `RFC-NNNN-short-slug.md` (zero-padded, monotonically increasing number).
- Status values: `Draft` → `Accepted` / `Rejected` → `Superseded`.
- Each RFC includes: Summary, Motivation, Detailed design, Drawbacks, Alternatives
  considered, Adoption / rollout, and Open questions.
